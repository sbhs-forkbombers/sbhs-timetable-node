"SBHS took too long"

This message can occur for a number of reasons - basically it means that the SBHS portal could not be contacted.
The most common causes will be:
  • Planned maintenance at SBHS - often there will be a post on the portal warning you before it happens (normally happens over weekends or holidays)
  • A buggy or broken reverse proxy, which can mean that the wider internet cannot access the school intranet (although you might be able to if you're at school)
  • A power outage at SBHS
  • An internet outage at SBHS
